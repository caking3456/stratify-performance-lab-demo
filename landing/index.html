Platform Features

Built to the clinical standard

Everything in the platform traces back to peer-reviewed biomechanics research — not bro-science.

🦴
Live Skeleton Animation
SVG-based skeletal visualization adapts per exercise with animated muscle overlays. Lerps between top and bottom positions frame-by-frame, showing exactly where load is during each phase.
⚡
Force Estimation Model
Simplified ground reaction force model estimates peak force in Newtons per rep, scaled to your body mass. Tracks fatigue-driven force decline across a set — a real metric, not a guess.
Escamilla et al. (2001, 2002) · GRF modeling basis
💪
Muscle Activation Map
Primary movers and stabilizers displayed with estimated activation percentages per exercise. Know whether your quads, glutes, or hamstrings are actually doing the work — or if your stabilizers are asleep.
⚠️
Fault Detection
Detects common movement faults like knee valgus, fatigue-driven form breakdown, and reduced depth. Flags the exact rep where quality drops — so you know when to stop, not just when to start.
Myer et al. (2008) · Ireland et al. (2003)
📋
HEP Corrective Prescription
After each set, the platform generates a Home Exercise Program — targeted corrective drills mapped to detected faults. Clamshells for valgus. Tempo squats for fatigue. Dowel drills for hinge pattern. Real PT logic.
Distefano et al. (2009) · Khayambashi et al. (2012)
📤
CSV Export
Export your full set data — rep forces, quality scores, fault flags — as a CSV. Track trends across weeks. Bring data to your coach, PT, or just keep it for yourself.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this a substitute for seeing a PT?
No — and we're clear about that. Stratify is an educational and training tool. It won't diagnose injuries or replace clinical evaluation. But it will help you train smarter between appointments, understand your movement patterns, and know when something's worth getting checked out.
Do I need any equipment or cameras?
None. Stratify runs entirely in your browser using a simulation engine — no wearables, no phone cameras, no setup. The MediaPipe real-camera integration is on the roadmap; the current platform gives you clinically-modeled simulation that's already more detailed than most consumer apps.
How accurate is the force estimation?
It's a simplified GRF (ground reaction force) model scaled to your body mass and the exercise's force multiplier, based on published biomechanics research. It won't match a force plate in a sports science lab — but it gives you a meaningful relative measure of output and fatigue trends across a set.
Who is this built for?
Serious athletes who self-coach, lifters who want to understand their mechanics beyond YouTube cues, gym-goers who've had injuries and want to train more intelligently, and anyone curious about what their body is actually doing under load.
What's the HEP prescription?
After each set, Stratify generates a Home Exercise Program — corrective drills targeted at detected faults. Knee valgus? You'll get clamshells and lateral band walks with DPT-written cues and evidence citations. It's the same logic a PT would apply, delivered after every session.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, always. No lock-ins, no cancellation fees. Your trial starts free with no credit card. If you upgrade and decide it's not for you, cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged.
